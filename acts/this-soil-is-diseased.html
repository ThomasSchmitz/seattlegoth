This Soil Is Diseased

Seattle, WA
Years Active: 2000s-2010s

Washington State Connection

This Soil Is Diseased emerged from Seattle's environmental and socially conscious dark music scene, reflecting the Pacific Northwest's deep concerns about ecological destruction and the relationship between industrial contamination and spiritual corruption.

This Soil Is Diseased represented the environmentally conscious and politically engaged side of Seattle's dark alternative scene, creating compositions that addressed themes of ecological destruction, corporate malfeasance, and the spiritual costs of environmental degradation. The project's provocative name captured their focus on the interconnection between physical and moral contamination, reflecting the Pacific Northwest's particular sensitivity to environmental issues.

Known for their sophisticated approach to combining environmental themes with dark electronic music, This Soil Is Diseased incorporated field recordings from industrial sites, processed environmental sounds, and atmospheric electronics to create immersive compositions that served as both artistic statement and environmental activism. Their work often featured themes of land use, water pollution, and the psychological effects of living in contaminated landscapes.

The project was part of Seattle's broader tradition of socially conscious alternative music, representing the intersection between environmental activism and dark artistic expression. This Soil Is Diseased demonstrated how Seattle artists were finding innovative ways to address serious political and environmental concerns through dark music, maintaining the city's reputation for producing art that engages with real-world issues while creating genuinely compelling aesthetic experiences.
