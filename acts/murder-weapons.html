Murder Weapons is a Seattle-based underground project that has been active in the city's dark alternative music scene since 2012. They represent the gore-rock and dark electronica elements of Seattle's diverse industrial and gothic music community, contributing to the city's reputation for innovative dark alternative music.

Murder Weapons is a Seattle-based underground gore-rock and electronica project that has been active since 2012, creating dark aesthetic-driven music that combines horror elements with electronic production. The project is known for its provocative imagery and sound design that pushes the boundaries of traditional gothic and industrial music into more extreme territories.

The project has gained recognition within the dark alternative music community, notably opening for established acts like My Life with the Thrill Kill Kult, which demonstrates their standing within the industrial and gothic music scene. This connection to legendary acts shows Murder Weapons' integration into the broader network of dark alternative music that spans from underground Seattle venues to international touring acts.

Their Bandcamp presence features tracks including "Pulling Teeth," "Frantic (X‑FUSION remix)," "GUILTY," and "Jigsaw," showcasing their range from aggressive original compositions to collaborative remix work. The X‑FUSION remix demonstrates their connections to the broader industrial music community and their willingness to reinterpret their work through different electronic production approaches.
