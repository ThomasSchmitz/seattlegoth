Washington State Connection

Ghost Fetish emerged from Seattle's vibrant post-punk and synth-pop revival scene, quickly establishing themselves as one of the city's most energetic live acts. The trio has become a fixture in Seattle's underground music venues, contributing to the Pacific Northwest's ongoing legacy of genre-blending alternative music.

Ghost Fetish is a vibrant synth-fueled post-punk trio from Seattle known for their dance-inducing live shows rooted in new wave, synth-pop, and classic goth influences. The band has carved out a distinctive niche in the Pacific Northwest music scene by combining the driving rhythms of post-punk with the melodic sensibilities of synth-pop, creating an infectious sound that fills dance floors across the region.

Their debut album "Almost Touching" was released in September 2023 to critical acclaim, showcasing the band's ability to blend nostalgic synth textures with contemporary production techniques. The album captures the essence of their high-energy live performances while demonstrating their songwriting maturity and attention to atmospheric detail. The release marked a significant milestone for the Seattle-based trio, establishing them as a force to be watched in the gothic and post-punk revival scenes.

Ghost Fetish has been particularly active with live performances throughout Washington State, building a dedicated following through their engaging stage presence and carefully crafted setlists that seamlessly blend original material with well-chosen covers. Their approach to performance emphasizes the communal aspect of gothic and post-punk culture, creating an inclusive atmosphere that welcomes both longtime fans of the genre and newcomers drawn to their accessible yet sophisticated sound.
