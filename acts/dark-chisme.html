Washington State Connection

Dark Chisme brings a unique multicultural perspective to Seattle's dark wave scene, representing the growing diversity of the Pacific Northwest gothic and industrial music community while maintaining strong ties to the city's underground electronic music culture.

Dark Chisme is a groundbreaking Seattle-based dark wave project that stands out in the Pacific Northwest music scene for its innovative fusion of traditional dark wave aesthetics with Latin, house, and techno influences. Led by Chicago-raised Latina DJ and producer Christine "DJ Gold Chisme" Gutierrez, who handles vocals and synthesizers, the project collaborates with the enigmatic musician known simply as "E," who contributes bass and additional synthesizer work to create their distinctive sound.

The project represents a significant evolution in dark wave music, demonstrating how the genre can be expanded and enriched through the incorporation of diverse cultural influences and electronic music styles. Christine's background as a DJ brings a deep understanding of rhythm and dancefloor dynamics to the project, while her Latina heritage infuses the music with cultural perspectives rarely heard in traditional gothic and industrial scenes. This combination creates music that maintains the atmospheric and emotional depth of classic dark wave while introducing fresh rhythmic and melodic elements.

Dark Chisme's work, including tracks like "Beautiful Obsession Killer" featured on the 2024 "Tremolo" release through Neon Sigh, showcases their ability to create music that works equally well in club environments and as more contemplative listening experiences. Their sound bridges the gap between the introspective nature of dark wave and the more kinetic energy of house and techno, creating a hybrid that reflects the multicultural nature of contemporary Seattle's music scene. Through their innovative approach, Dark Chisme is helping to redefine what dark wave can be in the 21st century.
