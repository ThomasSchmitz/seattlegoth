Strap on Halo is a Seattle-based goth-rock and darkwave act distinguished by haunting female vocals that serve as the driving force behind their atmospheric sound. The project represents the contemporary development of Seattle's gothic music scene, creating compositions that balance the darker aesthetic elements characteristic of goth music with the electronic production techniques associated with darkwave. This combination reflects the ongoing evolution of Seattle's dark alternative music community.
