Washington State Connection

Nox Novacula formed in Seattle in 2017 and quickly established themselves within the Gothic and deathrock scene as revitalizers of macabre artistry in the city. They represent the contemporary evolution of Seattle's gothic music scene and have become ambassadors for Pacific Northwest deathrock through extensive festival performances and international recognition.

Nox Novacula emerged from Seattle's gothic music scene in 2017, positioning themselves as deathrock pioneers dedicated to revitalizing macabre artistry within the contemporary gothic landscape. Their debut demo in 2018 made a notable impact within the genre, establishing them as significant contributors to the revival of classic deathrock aesthetics while incorporating modern production techniques and contemporary artistic approaches.

The band has gained recognition through extensive festival appearances at genre-defining events such as Near Dark, Out From the Shadows, and San La Muerte, demonstrating their appeal beyond the Pacific Northwest to national and international gothic music communities. Their festival circuit involvement has positioned them as ambassadors for Seattle's contemporary deathrock scene, connecting the city's gothic music tradition to broader global gothic music networks.

Their discography includes "The Beginning" (2019, Bat Cave Productions – Paris), "Ascension" (2021), "Moment of Pleasure" (2021), and their latest album "Feed the Fire" (August 2024, Artoffact Records). This most recent release blends deathrock and post-punk with electronic flourishes, featuring standout tracks "Wolves," "Sirens," and "Cursed." The album has received critical acclaim within the gothic music community, with fans describing it as exceptional contemporary deathrock. Recent touring includes Tacoma performances and upcoming dates at A Murder of Crows Festival (2025).
