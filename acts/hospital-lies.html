Washington State Connection

Hospital Lies operates from Washington State, contributing to the region's diverse dark electronic and alternative music scene. The duo represents the Pacific Northwest's tradition of boundary-pushing electronic music that blends aggressive and atmospheric elements, continuing the legacy established by acts like Assemblage 23 and Rabbit Junk.

Hospital Lies is an electro-rock duo featuring Joshua Scott on vocals and guitar and Kevin Preston handling synth and guitar duties. The band operates at the compelling intersection of dark electronic music and alternative rock, creating a sound that bridges the gap between aggressive industrial textures and more accessible rock sensibilities. Their approach to composition demonstrates a sophisticated understanding of both electronic and traditional rock instrumentation.

The duo's sound is characterized by Scott's distinctive vocal delivery paired with Preston's electronic textures and guitar work, creating a dynamic that sets them apart within Washington's dark alternative scene. Their music explores raw emotional themes, with compositions that balance electronic experimentation with rock song structures. This hybrid approach has positioned them as unique contributors to the state's growing reputation for innovative dark electronic music.

Their debut EP "All Hearts Bleed the Same" showcases the band's ability to craft emotionally resonant music that explores themes of vulnerability and human connection while maintaining the dark aesthetic that defines their sound. The band maintains an official presence through hospital-lies.com, serving as a hub for their musical activities and connecting with fans of Washington's dark electronic scene. Though they maintain a more underground presence compared to some of their Pacific Northwest contemporaries, Hospital Lies represents the continued evolution of the region's dark alternative music community.
