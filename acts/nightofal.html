NiGHTofAL is a Seattle-based synth/coldwave solo project that represents the contemporary evolution of the city's electronic underground music scene. NiGHTofAL is a Seattle-based synth and coldwave solo project known for hardware-driven electronic performances and atmospheric theatrics. The project creates original music that blends elements of electronic, metal, punk, and avant-garde genres, resulting in a unique sound that transcends traditional category boundaries while maintaining a distinctly dark electronic foundation.

The project's approach to live performance emphasizes atmospheric theatrics and hardware-driven electronic composition, creating immersive experiences that showcase the intersection of technology and artistry. This performance style reflects Seattle's tradition of innovative electronic artists who push beyond conventional presentation formats to create memorable live experiences.

NiGHTofAL maintains an active Bandcamp presence featuring notable tracks like "TEMPO69" (December 2021), which showcases their darkwave soundscapes. Their discography includes "escape from modern world" (2019), "ViSiON" (2020), "RiSE" (2020), and "LiVE At SOUTHGATE ROLLER RiNK EP" (2023), demonstrating consistent artistic development and a commitment to documenting their evolving electronic sound.
