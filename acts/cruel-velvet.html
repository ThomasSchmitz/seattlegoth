Washington State Connection

Cruel Velvet is an atmospheric goth project based in Spokane, Washington, representing the eastern Washington gothic scene and bringing dark wave aesthetics to the inland Pacific Northwest.

Cruel Velvet emerged from the shadows of Spokane in autumn 2018, founded by Josh Rodriguez and Taylor D. Waring after the two artists connected through the gothic community on VampireFreaks.com. The duo set out to create what they describe as "goth-scapes" - atmospheric soundscapes that weave together the melancholic beauty and haunting darkness that defines the gothic aesthetic.

The band's sound draws heavily from the influential early 2000s Projekt Records compilations, channeling that era's ethereal darkwave and atmospheric gothic music into their own contemporary vision. Their approach combines lush, layered soundscapes with haunting melodies and introspective lyrics, creating music that serves as both an escape and a meditation on darker themes.

Cruel Velvet released their debut "Demonstration" EP, establishing their presence in the Pacific Northwest gothic scene with tracks that showcase their ability to craft immersive atmospheric pieces. Songs like "Angelus," "I Never Feel Alive," and "Blank Planet" demonstrate their range within the gothic spectrum, from ethereal ambient pieces to more structured darkwave compositions. The project continues to develop their sound while maintaining their focus on creating beautiful, melancholic music that captures the essence of modern gothic expression.
