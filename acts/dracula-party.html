Dracula Party

Seattle, Washington
Years Active: 2019-present

Washington State Connection

Dracula Party is a Seattle-based horror-industrial project that emerged from the Pacific Northwest's thriving dark electronic scene. Led by Byron C. Miller, who has deep roots in Washington's gothic and industrial community through his work with God Module and Ghosts in the Graveyard, the project represents the contemporary evolution of Seattle's dark music legacy.

Dracula Party is a Seattle-based horror-industrial project led by Byron C. Miller (formerly of God Module) with Chris Staccato (Idoless) on production. Their music evokes classic horror movie atmospheres, creating a perfect blend of gothic and industrial theatrics that pays homage to the golden age of horror cinema while delivering cutting-edge electronic music.

The project represents Byron Miller's continued exploration of dark electronic music, building on his extensive experience in the Pacific Northwest scene. With Chris Staccato's production expertise, Dracula Party creates immersive soundscapes that transport listeners into a world of vintage horror aesthetics, complete with atmospheric samples, driving industrial beats, and gothic melodies that capture the essence of classic monster movies.

Their full discography is available on Bandcamp, with the standout album "Video Wasteland" (2021) showcasing their mastery of horror-themed industrial music. The project has established itself as a significant presence in Seattle's dark electronic scene, continuing the city's tradition of innovative genre-blending artists who push the boundaries of gothic and industrial music while maintaining deep connections to horror culture and cinematography.
