Years Active: 2016 - Present
Washington State Connection: Dark industrial/EBM project formed in Seattle in 2016, representing the contemporary evolution of the city's industrial music scene.

Black Agent is a dark industrial/EBM project from Seattle, formed in 2016 by Lawrence "Larry" Kleinke. What began as a solo project evolved into a multi-member act by 2019, incorporating Neil Parker on guitar, bass, and live drums, along with vocalist J Pit. Their sound evokes the classic 1980s industrial and EBM traditions while maintaining a distinctly modern and aggressive edge.

The band released their debut album "Industrial Ruination" in April 2022, followed by "Dehumanized" in 2023. Both albums are characterized by gritty sonic textures and explore themes of societal decay, personal loss, and mental unrest. Their music combines the mechanical precision of classic EBM with the harsh, grinding sounds of modern industrial, creating a sound that is both nostalgic and forward-looking.

Black Agent represents the ongoing evolution of Seattle's industrial music scene, bridging the gap between the city's legendary 1990s industrial movement and contemporary dark electronic music. Their work demonstrates how new artists continue to find fresh ways to express the themes of alienation and social critique that have always been central to industrial music.
