-ACCOLADE- is Seattle's ethereal darkwave outfit, fronted by classical singer Stefanie Reneé with multi-instrumentalist Aaron Goldstein. Their sound marries gothic neoclassical and industrial elements, creating lush, atmospheric dark music that stands apart in the Pacific Northwest's gothic landscape.

The band's approach combines Stefanie's classically-trained vocals with Aaron's multi-instrumental capabilities, crafting compositions that bridge the gap between gothic tradition and modern darkwave innovation. Their music creates immersive soundscapes that reflect both the beauty and darkness inherent in the gothic aesthetic.

-ACCOLADE- has maintained an active presence in Seattle's dark music community, contributing to the city's reputation as a hub for innovative gothic and industrial music. Their neoclassical influences set them apart from many of their contemporaries, offering a more orchestral and atmospheric approach to darkwave.
