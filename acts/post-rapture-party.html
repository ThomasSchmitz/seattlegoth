Post Rapture Party is a Seattle shoegaze, post-rock, and goth band that embodies the Pacific Northwest's characteristic atmospheric gloom and introspective musical approach. Their work represents the intersection of gothic and post-rock genres within Seattle's diverse alternative music scene, creating gothic atmospheres born specifically of Pacific Northwest environmental and cultural influences.

Post Rapture Party is a Seattle-based band that weaves together shoegaze, post-rock, and goth influences to create music centered around stories of loss, love, and ghostly themes. Described as the "love child of post-rock and psychedelic shoegaze," their sound incorporates gothic atmospheres that are directly influenced by Pacific Northwest gloom, making them distinctly representative of Seattle's environmental and cultural impact on dark alternative music.

The band has maintained an active presence in Seattle's live music scene, performing regularly across the city's key venues including Central Saloon, El Corazon, and Madame Lou's. Their live performances have featured notable pairings with other significant Seattle dark alternative acts including Licorice Chamber, Eve's Black Heart, Hexengeist, and Dracula Party, demonstrating their integration into the broader network of the city's gothic and alternative music community.

Their discography includes their self-titled debut album (2015) and additional releases available through their Bandcamp presence, which serves as the primary source for their biographical information and complete catalog. The band's approach to combining post-rock's instrumental complexity with shoegaze's atmospheric textures and gothic music's thematic content creates a distinctive sound that reflects both Seattle's musical traditions and contemporary alternative music development.
