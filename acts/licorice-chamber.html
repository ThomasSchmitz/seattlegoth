Licorice Chamber

Seattle, Washington
Years Active: 2020s - Present

Washington State Connection

Licorice Chamber represents the evolution of Seattle's darkwave scene through the solo work of Layla Reyna, formerly of the Seattle band Strap On Halo. Based in Seattle, the project continues the city's tradition of innovative dark electronic music while building connections with contemporary acts like 2Libras and Replicas through collaborative touring efforts.

Licorice Chamber emerges as the distinctive solo darkwave project of Layla Reyna, formerly a member of the Seattle band Strap On Halo. This transition from band member to solo artist represents a natural evolution in Reyna's musical journey, allowing her to explore a more personal and cinematic approach to darkwave composition. The project name itself evokes the complex, bittersweet nature of her musical explorations, suggesting both sweetness and darkness in equal measure, much like the confection that inspires its name.

The project's sound is characterized by a sophisticated blend of darkwave synthesizers and post-punk guitar elements, creating atmospheric compositions that bridge the gap between electronic and organic instrumentation. Reyna's approach to Licorice Chamber emphasizes cinematic qualities, crafting soundscapes that evoke film noir aesthetics and emotional landscapes. This cinematic quality has earned critical recognition, with her work being praised for its "gothic mystique" and its ability to create immersive auditory experiences that transport listeners into carefully constructed emotional territories.

Licorice Chamber's recent activity demonstrates both creative productivity and active engagement with the Seattle dark music community. Her 2022 EP "The Taste of Falling" received critical acclaim for its mature songwriting and atmospheric production, establishing her as a significant voice in contemporary darkwave. The 2024 "Sentience" EP continued this trajectory, leading to the announcement of the Grey Skies Tour with fellow Seattle acts Replicas and 2Libras. This collaborative touring effort showcases the interconnected nature of Seattle's current darkwave scene and positions Licorice Chamber as an important part of the city's ongoing dark alternative music narrative.
