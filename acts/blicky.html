Blicky

Seattle, WA
Years Active: 2005-present
Washington State Connection: Formed in Seattle in May 2005, representing the Pacific Northwest's post-punk and industrial dance rock underground scene with nearly two decades of local activity.

Blicky (stylized as "blicky") emerged from Seattle's vibrant underground music scene in May 2005 as a post-punk/new-wave/industrial dance rock band that has maintained a consistent presence in the Pacific Northwest's alternative music landscape for nearly two decades. The band's formation represented the convergence of multiple musical influences and personalities, each bringing their own instrumental skills and creative perspectives to the collective sound.

The band's original lineup featured Paul Johnson on electronic percussion and synthesizers, Shaun Mason handling guitar, bass, synths, vocals, and drum programming, and Samuel Blunk contributing synths, bass, guitar, and vocals. This multi-instrumental approach allowed the band to create complex, layered compositions that seamlessly blended electronic and organic elements, giving them the flexibility to explore various sonic territories within their post-punk and industrial dance framework.

Blicky's sound draws from the rich tradition of post-punk and new wave while incorporating industrial dance elements that reflect Seattle's diverse musical heritage. Their music combines driving rhythms, atmospheric synthesizers, and angular guitar work to create an energetic yet dark aesthetic that has resonated with audiences in the Pacific Northwest underground scene. This approach has allowed them to maintain relevance across multiple waves of musical trends while staying true to their core artistic vision.

In 2012, the band experienced a significant lineup change when Paul Johnson departed, leading to Aaron Nicholes joining the group. This transition marked a new chapter for Blicky while maintaining their core sound and creative vision. The band has maintained an active presence with multiple releases available on Bandcamp, and has built a following through social media platforms including Facebook, where they've shared both musical updates and community announcements, including commemorating the passing of founding member Samuel Blunk.

Throughout their career, Blicky has remained an active part of Seattle's alternative music scene, contributing to the city's reputation for fostering innovative and genre-blending musical projects. Their longevity speaks to both their musical quality and their connection to the local community that has supported them for nearly two decades. Their presence on platforms like ReverbNation and their continued activity demonstrate their commitment to reaching new audiences while honoring their underground roots.
