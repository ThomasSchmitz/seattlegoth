Datura

Wenatchee, Washington
Years Active: 2018 - Present

Washington State Connection

Datura represents the gothic and post-punk scene in central Washington, bringing the atmospheric darkness of classic goth-rock to Wenatchee and the inland Pacific Northwest, extending the reach of Washington's gothic music community beyond the major metropolitan areas.

Datura emerges from the unlikely gothic stronghold of Wenatchee, Washington, bringing post-punk and goth-rock sensibilities to the apple country of central Washington. The band draws deeply from the wellspring of classic gothic and post-punk influences, channeling the atmospheric genius of legendary acts like The Cure, Siouxsie & the Banshees, Bauhaus, The Chameleons, and early Dead Can Dance into their own contemporary vision of dark, melodic rock music.

The band's commitment to the gothic aesthetic goes beyond mere musical homage, creating compositions that capture both the melancholic beauty and the haunting intensity that defined the golden age of goth-rock. Their sound demonstrates a deep understanding of the genre's essential elements - the interplay of light and shadow, the balance between despair and hope, and the power of atmosphere to transport listeners to otherworldly emotional landscapes. This dedication to craft has resulted in music that feels both reverent to the tradition and genuinely contemporary.

Datura's discography includes early releases like the "Orphans" and "Bury Me" EPs from 2019-2020, which established their presence in the Pacific Northwest gothic scene, followed by the full-length "Arcano Chemical" LP which showcased their evolution as songwriters and their ability to create cohesive album-length statements. Their "Songs From a Dark House" collection demonstrates their range and their commitment to exploring the full emotional spectrum of gothic music. Through these releases, Datura has proven that the gothic spirit can flourish anywhere, bringing darkness and beauty to unexpected corners of Washington State.
