Washington State Connection

Lost Masters is a Spokane-based dark electronic project, representing the gothic and industrial music scene in Eastern Washington. The project showcases the diversity of Washington State's dark alternative music beyond the Seattle metropolitan area.

Lost Masters is a dark electronic project that merges aggressive beats with rich synthesizer atmospheres to deliver intense and nuanced soundscapes. The project builds from a dark electronic framework, creating music that explores the darker territories of electronic composition with precision and atmospheric depth.

Operating from Spokane, Lost Masters represents the Eastern Washington contribution to the state's gothic and industrial music scene. The project's approach to dark electronic music emphasizes both rhythmic intensity and atmospheric complexity, creating compositions that balance aggression with sophisticated sound design.

The project maintains an active presence on Bandcamp, where fans can access their catalog of dark electronic compositions. Lost Masters continues to develop their signature sound that combines the harder elements of industrial music with the atmospheric depth characteristic of darkwave and ambient electronic genres.
