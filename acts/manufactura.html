Manufactura

Los Angeles/Seattle Connections, Washington
Years Active: 2002-present

Washington State Connection

While based in Los Angeles, Manufactura maintains significant connections to the Pacific Northwest industrial scene, with ties to Seattle's underground electronic and noise music communities. The project's influence extends throughout the West Coast industrial network, including Washington State venues and collaborations.

Manufactura is an industrial rhythmic noise project created by Karloz.M, known for producing ritualistic, ambient, and transformative electronic compositions. The project has developed a global fanbase through its intense explorations of industrial sound design and rhythmic noise construction, often described as creating esoteric experiences for listeners.

The project's most recent major release, "Avulsion," represents their 7th studio album and is specifically intended for esoteric experiences like astral projection and lucid dreaming. This ambitious work was dedicated to the influential electronic group COIL, showcasing Manufactura's commitment to pushing the boundaries of consciousness through sound. The album exemplifies the project's approach to creating transformative auditory experiences.

In 2022, Manufactura celebrated a significant milestone with "Regression: 20th Anniversary Edition," a remastered release that included an eBook, poster, and retrospectives. This release is considered a classic within the noise genre and demonstrates the project's enduring influence on industrial and rhythmic noise music. The comprehensive reissue reflects both the historical importance of the original work and its continued relevance to contemporary noise music practitioners.
