Cylab

Seattle, Washington
Years Active: 2007 - Present

Washington State Connection

Cylab represents the cutting-edge of Seattle's electronic music scene, blending industrial and darkwave elements with experimental electronic music, contributing to the Pacific Northwest's reputation for boundary-pushing dark electronic art.

Cylab is an ambitious electronic music project formed by Percy Trayanov, who serves as the primary architect of the band's genre-defying sound. Collaborating with talented artists including Dre Robinson and Severina Sol, Cylab has established itself as a boundary-breaking force in the dark electronic music landscape, seamlessly weaving together elements of electro, industrial, IDM, EBM, darkwave, and synth music into a cohesive and innovative sonic experience.

The project's approach to electronic music production showcases a deep understanding of both classic and contemporary electronic music forms. By drawing from the rich traditions of industrial and EBM while incorporating modern IDM and darkwave aesthetics, Cylab creates compositions that feel both familiar and refreshingly new. Their sound demonstrates technical sophistication while maintaining the emotional depth and atmospheric intensity that defines the best dark electronic music.

Cylab's 2010 album "Cut & Coil" stands as a testament to their artistic vision, featuring detailed tracklists and lyrical content that explores themes common to the industrial and darkwave scenes. The album showcases the project's ability to create complete artistic statements that work both as individual tracks and as cohesive listening experiences. Through releases like this, Cylab continues to push the boundaries of what dark electronic music can be, contributing to Seattle's legacy as a center for innovative and experimental music.
