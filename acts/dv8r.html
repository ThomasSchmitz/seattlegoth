DV8R is a Seattle-based dark electro/industrial trio that emerged from the Pacific Northwest's thriving electronic music underground. Formed in 2016, the band has become an active part of Seattle's contemporary dark electronic scene, representing a new generation of artists carrying forward the city's industrial music legacy.

DV8R is a Seattle-based dark electro/industrial trio formed in 2016, comprised of Corey Wittenborn, Matt Sharifi, and Kevin Preston. The band emerged from Seattle's vibrant electronic underground, bringing a fresh perspective to the dark electro genre while maintaining deep connections to the city's industrial music heritage. Their sound combines driving electronic beats with atmospheric textures, creating music that appeals to both dancefloor enthusiasts and more contemplative listeners.

The trio's approach to dark electro demonstrates both technical proficiency and artistic vision, crafting tracks that balance aggressive electronic elements with melodic sophistication. Their music reflects the Pacific Northwest's tradition of genre-blending innovation, incorporating influences from EBM, synthwave, and industrial while maintaining a distinctly modern edge. The band's collaborative approach, with all three members contributing to their sound, creates a rich sonic palette that sets them apart in the contemporary dark electronic scene.

DV8R has gained recognition through their association with Infacted Recordings, a respected label in the dark electronic community. Their notable releases include "IO" (2021), which showcased their evolving sound and established them as a significant presence in the international dark electro scene. The band continues to be active in the Seattle music community while building a following beyond the Pacific Northwest, representing the ongoing vitality of Washington State's electronic music scene.
