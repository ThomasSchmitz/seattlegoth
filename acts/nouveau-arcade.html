Nouveau Arcade

Seattle, Washington
Years Active: 2021-present

Washington State Connection

Nouveau Arcade is a Seattle-based duo that represents the contemporary synthwave and retrowave scene in the Pacific Northwest. Their work contributes to Seattle's diverse electronic music landscape, blending nostalgic synthwave aesthetics with modern production techniques to create music that bridges the city's electronic music past and present.

Nouveau Arcade is a Seattle-based duo that blends modwave, synthwave, cyberpunk, and retrowave influences to create neon-soaked electronic compositions with a distinctly nostalgic flair. Their music captures the aesthetic of 1980s electronic music while incorporating modern production techniques and contemporary songwriting approaches, resulting in a sound that feels both familiar and innovative.

The duo's approach to electronic composition emphasizes visual and sonic aesthetics that evoke cyberpunk and retro-futuristic themes. Their music combines lush synthesizer work with driving rhythms, creating compositions that work equally well for active listening and background atmosphere. This visual-sonic integration reflects the contemporary synthwave movement's emphasis on multimedia artistic expression.

Their Bandcamp presence features "Dead Hearts" (2023), a full album that mixes goth disco, summer synth, and cyber rhythms, demonstrating their ability to blend different electronic subgenres into a cohesive artistic statement. Additional releases include singles like "Fluorescent" (2022), "Stronger Now" (2022) with Signal Void, "Jukebox Cake" (2021), and collaborative work "Apart" featuring Cordélia (2022), showcasing their versatility and collaborative spirit within the electronic music community.
